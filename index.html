Inline video playback for iPhone / iPod

This demo shows inline video playback on iPhone devices. The following features are fully supported:

  • Audio sync
  • Progressive playback (.mp4 videos)
  • HLS streaming
  • Live streaming
  • Seek action
  • Custom controls
